By "augmenting human intellect" we mean increasing the capability of a man to approach a complex problem situation, to g
ain comprehension to suit his particular needs, and to derive solutions to problems. Increased capability in this respec
t is taken to mean a mixture of the following: more-rapid comprehension, better comprehension, the possibility of gainin
g a useful degree of comprehension in a situation that previously was too complex, speedier solutions, better solutions,
 and the possibility of finding solutions to problems that before seemed insoluble. And by "complex situations" we inclu
de the professional problems of diplomats, executives, social scientists, life scientists, physical scientists, attorney
s, designers—whether the problem situation exists for twenty minutes or twenty years. We do not speak of isolated clever
tricks that help in particular situations. We refer to a way of life in an integrated domain where hunches, cut-and-try,
intangibles, and the human "feel for a situation" usefully co-exist with powerful concepts, streamlined terminology and
notation, sophisticated methods, and high-powered electronic aids.

Augmenting Human Intellect: A Conceptual Framework, By: Douglas C. Engelbart



“提高人类智能”是指增加一个人的能力来解决一个复杂问题,以获得适合他的特殊需要的能力,以及获得解决问题的方法。增加这方面的能
力是指以下事项:更快的理解力,更好的领悟力,对于以前很复杂的问题，能够获得有效的理解程度,更快、更好的解决方案,在似乎无法解
决问题之前，找到解决问题的可能性。“复杂情况下”我们包括外交官、高管、社会学家、生命学家、物理学家、律师、设计师等专业问题
——无论这些问题存在20分钟或20年。我们不谈论孤立的在特定情况下有帮助的聪明的技巧。我们指的是一种生活方式，在一个综合范围的
直觉、尝试、内省，以及人们“情形感知”有用的强大观念共存,简化的术语和符号,复杂的方法,和高性能的电子辅助设施。

出处：《提高人类智能:一个概念性的框架》 作者：Douglas C. Engelbart 译者：三岛(@译文网)
